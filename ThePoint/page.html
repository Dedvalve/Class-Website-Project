Curated Experience Description

Spend an evening learning to make sushi from world renowned Sushi Chef, Toshi Moroe. Toshi comes from the ever popular, Sushi Samba, so you know the experience will be top notch. The evening will start out with a group lesson in Toshi's best sushi techniques. It's not all business though, as guests will all the while be welcomed to enjoy a premium open bar as well as wine and imported/craft beers. After the lesson is over, guests will have fun perfecting their new talent and enjoying the fruits of their labor (well, the sushi of their labor).
